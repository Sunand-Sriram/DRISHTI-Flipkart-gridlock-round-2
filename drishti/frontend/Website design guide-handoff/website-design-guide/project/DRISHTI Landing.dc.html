Built for real enforcement,
real streets

DRISHTI combines computer vision, real-time pipelines, and a citizen-first portal into one unified enforcement system.

AI Camera Detection
Real-time YOLOv8 detects no-helmet, red-light, phone-use, and seatbelt violations across all feeds simultaneously.
Predictive Analytics
ML-powered hotspot prediction gives officers a 6-hour lookahead of violation-prone junctions based on historical data.
Instant e-Challans
Violations auto-generate challans linked to VAHAN data. SMS to registered owner within seconds — zero manual entry.
Live Map & Heatmap
Interactive city map with camera feeds, officer positions, and color-coded violation heatmaps for tactical deployment decisions.
Citizen Appeal Flow
Citizens contest challans with photo evidence. Officer review queue handles appeals within 5 business days with full audit trail.
Claude AI Analytics
Officers ask natural-language queries — "Which junction had the most violations?" — Claude responds with charts and insights instantly.
